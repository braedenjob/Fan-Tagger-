# FacilityTag

A mobile-first facility management app for tagging and tracking rooftop HVAC equipment.

## Features
- GPS satellite map with tap-to-draw equipment tags
- PM tickets with priority, assignment, and work logging
- PM scheduling and history tracking
- Crew management with shifts and employee assignments
- Multi-site support
- CSV export

## Usage
Open the app, navigate to your facility, and tap to place tags on equipment.
